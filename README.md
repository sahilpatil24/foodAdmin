🍽️ MealLink – Food Rescue Platform

MealLink is a platform designed to --reduce food waste by connecting restaurants with NGOs-- that can distribute surplus food to people in need.

The system enables restaurants to --donate leftover food efficiently--, while NGOs can quickly locate available food resources and coordinate pickups.

MealLink was developed during a --24-hour hackathon by Team NULL POINTERS--, where we focused on building a real-world solution for --food redistribution and social impact--.

 🌍 Problem

Food waste is a massive global issue.

- Restaurants and events often generate --large amounts of surplus food--
- NGOs struggle to --find reliable sources of donations--
- There is --no streamlined platform-- connecting food donors with organizations that can distribute it

As a result, --perfectly edible food is wasted while many people go hungry--.

---

 💡 Solution

MealLink acts as a --bridge between food donors and NGOs--, enabling fast and reliable redistribution of surplus food.

The platform allows:

- Restaurants to post available food donations
- NGOs to discover nearby food sources
- Secure coordination for food pickup and distribution

This helps reduce food waste and ensures surplus food reaches people who need it.

---

 🚀 Features

 🏪 Restaurant Portal

- Restaurants can register on the platform
- Post available surplus food
- Specify quantity and pickup time

 🤝 NGO Portal

- NGOs can browse available food donations
- Request food pickups
- Coordinate with restaurants easily

 🔐 Verification System

To maintain trust and authenticity:

- NGOs must go through a --verification process--
- Restaurants are also verified before listing donations
- Ensures legitimate organizations participate in the platform

 📦 Donation Management

- Track available food donations
- Manage pickup requests
- Update donation status
